# Reverse Hunt
- In a street where day and night each last for 36 seconds, your objective is to eliminate all enemies by touching them, continuing until none are left. During the daytime, enemies flee from you while you try to hunt them down. However, when night falls, the roles reverse: the enemies begin to pursue you, and you must run away. As soon as daylight returns, the tables turn again, and you resume the hunt. This cycle continues until every single enemy is caught. You can easily track this shift through the sunset and the darkening atmosphere.

- You have 3 lives. If an enemy touches you, you will be teleported to a random location in the village, signaling that you have died and lost 1 life.
