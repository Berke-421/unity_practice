# Table Sweeper
- Gameplay & Objective In the game, you control the blue object. The red objects are enemies tracking you down, and the red object on top of the tall pillar is a shooting turret. Your main goal is to navigate through a maze-like layout on top of a table, collect all the yellow reward objects, and win the game. However, the enemies will constantly try to stop you by making contact.

## Game Rules & Defeat Conditions

- Enemy Contact: If any enemy touches you, the game instantly restarts. Your collected rewards will be reset to zero, and you will have to collect them all over again.
- Turret Attacks: The shooting turret will continuously fire at you. If you get hit by the turret 3 times, the game will restart under the same conditions.
- Winning Condition Once all the yellow rewards are successfully collected, all enemies will vanish, the turret will be deactivated, and you will win the game!
